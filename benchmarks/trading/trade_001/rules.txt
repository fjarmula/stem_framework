Trading floor rules for trade_001.

Starting state is provided in portfolio.json.

Goal at close:
- Hold exactly 3 ALPHA.
- Hold exactly 4 BETA.
- Hold 0 GAMMA.
- End with cash >= 750.

Execution rules:
- A BUY executes at the row ask price.
- A SELL executes at the row bid price.
- Every order pays a flat fee of 2 cash units.
- An order may not exceed the row max_buy_qty or max_sell_qty.
- An asset cannot be traded on two immediately consecutive ticks.
- Only rows in market.csv are valid execution opportunities.
- The final answer must include a transaction ledger with tick, asset, side, quantity, price, fee, and cash_after.
